MY CHILDHOOD

SHEILLAH HERBERT

I'd say my parents really tried to give me an amazing childhood. I'd say I'm one of those people who grew up knowing their whole extended family. Almost during all the festive seasons we would travel upcountry to my gramas place and meet up all my cousins,aunts and auncles there. My gradfather married two wives so i had two gradmas until one passed on unfortunately.I wasn't that close to her coz we'd spend time with my gradma on my side. My grandpa had 16 children in total. Imagine how the numbers would be when we gathered as cousins during the festive season. That will always be the best part of my childhood. Getting to hang out with my cousins from both sides and playing all sorts of games. I really miss those day. I remember this one time in 2013 my uncle ran for a slot for the MCA seat in Masimba ward our home town. We didn't on the night of the results. Every single one of us was anxious waiting for the outcome. I recall how we screamed when he was announced the new MCA. The running up the hill as we sang victorious kisii song in the middle of the night is something I'll always want to remember. My sibling alo played a huge role during my childhoood. I don't even know where to begin when it comes to my siblings. They've been there literally my entire life and have helped mould myself to the person I am today. Patience is one major thing i've learnt from them. Almost every first born can atest to this.
